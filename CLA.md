## How to sign

Contributions are only merged from contributors who have agreed to this CLA.
Choose one of the following:

### Option A — Individual CLA (you contribute as yourself)

Sign electronically via the CLA bot on your first pull request (recommended;
we use the [CLA Assistant](https://cla-assistant.io) GitHub app), **or** add
your name to `contributors/individual.md` in a pull request with the statement:

> I have read the Lightning Mesh Contributor License Agreement and I hereby
> agree to its terms for my present and future Contributions to Lightning Mesh.
>
> Name: <your full legal name>
> GitHub: <@handle>
> Email: <email>
> Date: <YYYY-MM-DD>

### Option B — Corporate CLA (you contribute on behalf of an employer)

If you are contributing as part of your employment, an authorized
representative of your employer must agree to this CLA on the company's behalf
and list the employees authorized to contribute. Email **duke@identikey.io**
with:

> Company: <legal entity name>
> Authorized signatory: <name, title>
> Authorized contributors (GitHub handles): <list>
>
> On behalf of the company above, I have read the Lightning Mesh Contributor
> License Agreement and agree to its terms for the company's present and
> future Contributions to Lightning Mesh.
>
> Signature: <name>
> Date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
